西の魔女が死んだ

Author: 梨木 香歩

Mai, a middle school student, looks back on the time when she lived with her grandmother instead of going to school.

This novel reminded me of a Ghibli movie with all its descriptions of the beauty of everyday life and the work and tranquility in the garden.

Languagewise, it is rather short and a lot of the vocabulary is related to everyday life. However, it also introduces many specialised words for a lot of plants which I mostly skipped over.

Story is a bit slow but is a beautiful read for the N3+ level readers. 6.5/10 Buy here
