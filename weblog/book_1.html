また、同じ夢を見ていた

Author : 住野 よる

This is my review of the first japanese short novel book I completed. It took me about 50 days to finish while looking up words I didn't recognize.

The pleasant tone and characters meant I never got close to giving up, I found it a good book for early immersion.

While on the surface easy and repetitive, the amount of introspection, wordplay, and deliberation, makes the story enjoyable if the reader is actively on board the journey alongside the 主人公. There is a fair bit of realization and revelation in the last 20-30% of the book.

While the story isn't super groundbreaking or complex, it uses a good amount of figurative language to challenge your language skills a bit.

  • The girl thinks about what she will say and what others will say and considers the situation so there is some natural repetition of similar sentences making it great for language learning
  • There are humorous sentences and scenes maintaining entertainment

A great first read experience with an even better ending. 8/10 Buy here
